Privacy Policy

Dear Dates

Last Updated: 20-01-2026

Introduction

Welcome to Dear Dates ("we," "our," or "the app").

Your privacy is a core value of Dear Dates. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our iOS application.

Our Privacy Commitment

Dear Dates is designed to work without collecting, tracking, or transmitting your personal data.

All information you enter stays on your device and remains fully under your control.

Information We Store (Locally on Your Device)

Dear Dates stores all data locally on your iOS device only. We do not operate servers and do not sync or transmit your data.

Local Data Storage

The app may store the following information on your device:

  • • Profile Information: names, photos, notes, and other details you choose to save
  • • Event Information: birthdays, anniversaries, and custom events
  • • Gift Information: gift ideas, notes, and history
  • • User Profile: your own profile information, if you create one
  • • App Settings: theme preferences, accent colors, and notification settings
  • • Notification Data: minimal notification information (event names, dates, reminder days) stored separately for notification delivery
  • • App Preferences: language settings, onboarding completion status, and other preferences stored using iOS UserDefaults

All data is stored using SwiftData, Apple's native data persistence framework, and UserDefaults for app preferences.

We do not access, transmit, back up, or sync this data to any external servers or cloud services.

Photo Library Access

If you choose to add photos to profiles, Dear Dates may request access to your Photo Library.

This access:

  • • Is requested only when you tap "Add Photo"
  • • Is used solely to select photos for profiles inside the app
  • • Copies selected photos into the app's local storage
  • • Does not share photos with any external services

You can revoke photo access at any time in iOS Settings.

Notifications

Dear Dates uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming events.

These notifications:

  • • Are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device
  • • Do not use servers or external services
  • • Do not transmit any personal data
  • • Can be disabled at any time in the app settings or iOS Settings
  • • Require minimal data (event names, dates, reminder days) to be stored separately for delivery when your device is locked
  • • Use iOS file protection mechanisms and are accessible only after your device is first unlocked following a restart

Information We Do Not Collect

Dear Dates does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • • Analytics data
  • • Usage statistics
  • • Third-party analytics
  • • Advertising identifiers
  • • Device identifiers
  • • Location data
  • • Tracking data
  • • Third-party tracking information
  • • Personally identifiable information outside your device

Dear Dates does not use third-party crash reporting services and does not collect crash data outside Apple's standard development and distribution tools.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS security mechanisms.

This means:

  • • Your data never leaves your device through Dear Dates
  • • Your main database is encrypted by iOS using NSFileProtectionComplete and is accessible only when your device is unlocked
  • • Notification-related data is stored separately using NSFileProtectionCompleteUntilFirstUserAuthentication to ensure reminders work correctly
  • • Your data may be included in encrypted device backups managed by Apple, depending on your iOS settings
  • • Dear Dates does not access, control, or manage these backups

All file protection and encryption are handled by iOS and follow Apple's security best practices.

Data Sharing and Transmission

Dear Dates does not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to:

  • • Third-party services
  • • Advertising networks
  • • Analytics providers
  • • Cloud storage providers
  • • External servers or databases

The only way your data can leave your device is if you explicitly export it using the app's export feature.

Data Export and Deletion

You remain in full control of your data.

You can:

  • • Export all your data at any time as a JSON file using the app's export feature
  • • Import previously exported data using the app's import feature
  • • Search and filter your data within the app (all processing is performed locally)
  • • Delete individual profiles, events, or gift ideas
  • • Remove all data by uninstalling the app
  • • Reset app preferences by uninstalling and reinstalling the app

Third-Party Services

Dear Dates does not use third-party services that collect or transmit personal data.

The app uses only Apple-provided frameworks, including:

  • • SwiftUI
  • • SwiftData
  • • UserNotifications
  • • Photos
  • • Foundation
  • • UserDefaults

No advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or cloud synchronization services are used.

Children's Privacy

Dear Dates is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information within the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date and making the revised policy available in the app or online.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • • Access all data stored in the app
  • • Export your data at any time
  • • Delete your data at any time
  • • Control photo access via iOS Settings
  • • Control notification permissions via app settings or iOS Settings

International Users

This Privacy Policy is intended to align with major privacy regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA, to the extent applicable.

Because all data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted, Dear Dates provides a high level of privacy protection regardless of your location.

Conclusion

Dear Dates is built with privacy at its core.

We don't collect your data. We don't track you. We don't share your information.

Your memories stay private — exactly where they belong: on your device.
